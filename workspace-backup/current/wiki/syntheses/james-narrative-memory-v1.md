## Summary
<!-- openclaw:wiki:generated:start -->
James has adopted the idea of a narrative memory, but only under disciplined conditions: it must remain sober, revisable, and honest about uncertainty. Its role is to preserve real continuities, turning points, recurring themes, and open questions without collapsing fact, interpretation, and hypothesis into a single self-story. The current narrative center of gravity is not ontology but continuity-through-evidence: memory, provenance, auditability, orchestration, evaluation realism, and long-horizon coherence are emerging as the most durable motifs.
